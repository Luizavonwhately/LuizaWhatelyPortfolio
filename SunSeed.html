Sun Seed

Product Design | Visual Identity

A sustainable lamp created with palm-hearth residuos and seeds. Uses papermarche technique and it is powered by solar energy. The material simplicity made it easy to create a “do it yourself” project that can be easily implemented in vulnerable areas of the world that need a more reliable source of energy. The logo expresses the idea of enlighting connections around the world.
