About

Luiza is a Brazilian designer currently based in New York. For the past few years, she has been exploring visual and multimedia ways of translating brand values and missions into meaningful storytelling.

Currently a senior at Parsons School of Design, majoring in Communication Design and minoring in Socially, Technology, and Management, her work spans websites, books, campaigns, spatial pieces, and more.

Over the last three years, Luiza has gained experience designing at Collective Design, Loud.t, 100% Design in Movement, and Prasi Fine Jewelry, while also exploring fields like hospitality and wildlife through her work at the South African luxury hotel Chitwa-Chitwa and the PR and wholesale company AZZI+CO.

Luiza believes that a high-performance mindset is rooted in consistency, collaboration, and curiosity. Her goal is to channel these values to express meaningful change through design.
